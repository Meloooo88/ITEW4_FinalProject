Welcome to Cabuyao Plaza

Cabuyao Plaza is the vibrant civic center of the city, serving as a public gathering space where culture, government, and daily life converge. Located near key municipal buildings, the plaza is often the venue for public ceremonies, community celebrations, and recreational activities. It features open green spaces, park benches, a performance stage, and occasionally exhibits showcasing local art and history. On weekends and holidays, families, students, and elderly residents gather here to relax, socialize, or enjoy local food sold by nearby vendors. The plaza also holds historical and symbolic value, reflecting Cabuyao’s transformation from a humble town into a progressive city. It remains a focal point of urban life and a symbol of civic pride for every Cabuyeño.
