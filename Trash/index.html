    Welcome to Discover Cabuyao

    Cabuyao is a vibrant city with a rich historical background. Once the central part of "Tabuco," a vast territory that included modern-day cities of San Pedro, Biñan, Santa Rosa, and Calamba, Cabuyao derived its name from the kabuyaw tree (Citrus hystrix).

    Following the colonization of Manila by Miguel López de Legazpi in 1570, the foundations for what would become Cabuyao were established. Today, the city blends historical charm with modern development, offering visitors a unique experience of Filipino culture, natural beauty, and local hospitality.
