Welcome to Saint Jerome Integrated School of Cabuyao

Saint Jerome Integrated School of Cabuyao, situated in Cabuyao, Laguna, is a distinguished institution offering elementary and high school education. Committed to fostering academic excellence and character development, the school provides a nurturing environment where students can thrive. With a dedicated faculty and modern facilities, Saint Jerome Integrated School emphasizes a well-rounded education that includes a strong academic curriculum, extracurricular activities, and community involvement. The school prepares its students to become responsible, knowledgeable, and compassionate individuals ready to succeed in their future endeavors.
