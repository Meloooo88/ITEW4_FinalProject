Welcome to Daang Nia

Daang Nia, located in Cabuyao, Laguna, is a beautiful farm spot known for its lush greenery and diverse array of plants. This serene destination offers visitors a peaceful retreat where they can immerse themselves in nature's beauty. Perfect for nature lovers and those seeking a tranquil escape, Daang Nia provides a picturesque setting ideal for leisurely walks, photography, and relaxation. Experience the natural charm and tranquility of Daang Nia, a hidden gem in the heart of Cabuyao.
