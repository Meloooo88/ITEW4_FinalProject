Welcome to SVCC

Saint Vincent College of Cabuyao, located in Cabuyao, Laguna, is dedicated to providing high-quality education grounded in strong values and academic excellence. The college offers a variety of programs designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed for professional success. Known for its supportive learning environment, Saint Vincent College emphasizes holistic development through a blend of rigorous academics, community involvement, and extracurricular activities.
