Welcome to Laguna Lake Marinig Shoreline

Laguna Lake Marinig Shoreline, also known as Laguna de Bay, is the largest lake in the Philippines and forms a breathtaking natural border along the eastern side of Cabuyao. Its vast expanse of calm, freshwater stretches into the horizon, providing not just a scenic view but also sustaining the livelihoods of local fishermen and farmers. The lake is central to many environmental and economic activities, such as aquaculture, irrigation, and even transport in nearby areas. Beyond its practical uses, Laguna Lake is a haven for relaxation, where locals enjoy fishing, boating, or simply watching the sunset. It also plays a critical role in ecological balance and flood control, making it an essential part of Cabuyao’s identity and environmental landscape.
