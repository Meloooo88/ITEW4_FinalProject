Welcome to Gulod National Highschool

Gulod National High School is a prominent public secondary school in Cabuyao that plays a vital role in shaping the future of local youth through quality education and community-oriented programs. It caters to students from diverse backgrounds and is known for its strong academic curriculum, dedicated faculty, and various extracurricular activities that support holistic development. The school emphasizes the importance of discipline, responsibility, and critical thinking, preparing its learners to become competent and morally upright citizens. With its expanding facilities and active engagement in both local and national educational initiatives, Gulod National High School continues to be a beacon of learning and progress in Cabuyao.
