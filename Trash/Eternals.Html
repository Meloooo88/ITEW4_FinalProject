Welcome to Cabuyao Eternals Gardens

Cabuyao Eternal Gardens is a beautifully landscaped memorial park that provides a solemn and tranquil resting place for departed loved ones. Situated in a peaceful area of the city, the park is thoughtfully designed with wide walking paths, well-kept lawns, and scenic views that offer a sense of calm and reflection. It serves as more than just a cemetery—it’s a sacred space for families to gather, honor their ancestors, and celebrate life. The Eternal Gardens has become an integral part of Cabuyao’s community traditions, especially during events like All Saints’ Day, where families come together to clean, decorate, and remember. With its dignified ambiance and respectful management, it remains a place of enduring memory and cultural significance.
