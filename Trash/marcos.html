Welcome to Marcos Mansion

The Marcos Mansion in Cabuyao, Laguna is a large, enigmatic structure tied to the legacy of the late President Ferdinand Marcos and his family, often shrouded in mystery and local intrigue. Though unoccupied and partially unfinished, the mansion’s grand design—with sprawling grounds, towering gates, and lavish architectural elements—hints at the former president’s taste for opulence during his regime. Situated in a quiet area of Cabuyao, it has become an object of fascination for locals and visitors alike, many of whom are drawn by stories of its abandoned halls and its association with the wealth and controversy of the Marcos era. Over time, the mansion has transformed from a symbol of political power to a historical curiosity, prompting debates about legacy, excess, and the forgotten footprints of history in Laguna. Despite its dormant state, it remains a striking landmark that stirs reflection on the city’s—and the nation’s—complex past.
